THE LAUNCH OF SPORTSWORK: CREATING A CENTRALISED HUB FOR ALL SPORTING JOBS

Client:

SportsWork

Industry:

Jobs and Recruitment

Website:

SportsWork.co

Tools:

Figma, CSS, JQuery, Google Analytics, and more.

Project Overview:

I played a key role in the design process for Sportswork, a sports job marketplace initiated by David Humphries and Johnny Small. My contribution spanned the creation of a dynamic design system, an intuitive homepage, and an optimised jobs listing page, and I also brought brand consistency across social media platforms with well-crafted templates and assets.

Problem Statement:

The sports industry needed a centralised platform for job seekers. The challenge was to provide an effective liaison between job seekers and employers, generating sustainable revenue via partnerships and premium job postings.

Design and Implementation:

Design Process:

The branding strategy was focused on energy and movement, leading to the selection of electric orange as the brand's main colour. I designed a user-friendly jobs listing page, a forum discussion, homepage, campaign pages, and a partner page. I also created a streamlined job posting flow and tiered job listings to promote profitability.

User Experience and Backend Design:

My design contributions extended beyond front-end, involving significant backend elements like account settings, helping users to manage their listings and jobs efficiently. A major UX improvement came with the redesign of the 404 page. It was transformed into a resourceful spot with email inputs, related job suggestions, and easy navigation, boosting site-wide conversions by 100%.

Implementation:

Part of my role also involved CSS implementation for the site, ensuring alignment between the design vision and the final product.

Results:

The platform launched successfully in 2021, showing financial independence and attracting multiple investment rounds. User engagement and experience saw significant improvement, validating the effectiveness of the design decisions.

Conclusion:

This project underscored the significance of user feedback in design iterations and the critical role of design in solving real-world problems, driving profitability, and enhancing user experience.
