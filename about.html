Who am I?

I'm a UX/UI designer with a passion for design and a keen interest in technology. I create scalable solutions that streamline processes and optimise results. I've worked on projects across diverse industries, collaborating with leading organisations like GSMA and CityFibre.

I understand that designing an exceptional user experience is more than just aesthetics; it's about providing a solution that's engaging, accessible, resonant, and relationship-building.

I work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure that the final design solution harmoniously aligns business objectives with the promise of a memorable user experience.

Outside of work, I have a keen interest in learning and helping teach martial arts, including Judo, traditional, and Brazilian jiu-jitsu. I also enjoy fishkeeping and aquariums. These hobbies help me to relax and de-stress, and they also teach me valuable lessons about discipline, patience, focus, and perseverance.
