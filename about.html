Who am I?

I'm a Product Designer with over 5 years of experience building thoughtful, user-centred digital products. I’ve worked on internal tools, SaaS platforms, and low-code systems, designing solutions that simplify complex workflows and deliver real impact.

I currently work at Budibase, where I lead design on AI features, automation tools, and platform improvements that make app-building faster and more accessible. Before that, I worked at Dawson Andrews, designing large-scale digital products for clients like CityFibre and GSMA. My work has helped boost user satisfaction, increase conversions, and align design with strategy.

I believe great product design is about more than clean UI. It’s about solving the right problems with clarity, purpose, and empathy. I enjoy working from early research through to final handoff, collaborating with cross-functional teams to turn ideas into well-executed products.

Outside of work, I hold a black belt in traditional Jiu-Jitsu, a blue belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and a green belt in Judo. I also enjoy fishkeeping, aquascaping, karting, and love dogs.
