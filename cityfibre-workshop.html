MEASURING CITYFIBRE PORTAL UX AND RESULTS: INNOVATING UX TESTING APPROACHES WITH AI AND AGILE METHODOLOGIES

Client:

CityFibre

Industry:

Broadband Infrastructure

Website:

The CityFibre Unify Portal

Tools:

Typeform, Figma, and ChatGPT.

Project Overview:

Taking on a multi-faceted role, I was assigned by CityFibre to lead and manage a crucial UX workshop. Facing time and budget constraints, I found innovative ways to utilise Typeform, Figma, and the AI-driven ChatGPT to boost the user experience for prominent ISPs like Vodafone, TalkTalk, and Zen.

Problem Statement:

CityFibre's goal was to elevate their backend portal's user experience, a crucial tool employed by leading ISPs for booking engineers to carry out home fibre installation surveys. With these high-profile clients, the pressure was on to deliver a top-tier UX testing experience.

Approach and solution:

Part 1 - UX Survey:

As a designer, I formulated an interactive survey using Typeform, Figma, and ChatGPT, which guided users through the website while posing vital usability questions. This strategy provided actionable insights akin to an AB test.

Part 2 - Interactive Feedback Sessions:

In addition to managing the project, I set up feedback sessions, showcasing the latest portal designs to the ISPs, and actively sought their opinions on new potential features. These sessions offered invaluable suggestions that significantly enhanced the portal's user experience.

UX Test Execution:

For the execution phase, we invited ISPs to CityFibre's offices to experience the new system first-hand. I managed the workshop by dividing the participants into teams, with each team independently navigating through the survey without any complications, thus validating our innovative methodology.

User Experience Improvements:

I enhanced key user journeys, such as postcode availability checking, resulting in a seamless and intuitive user experience. By ensuring accessibility and maintaining visual appeal, I created a design that catered to a diverse user base.

Final Solution:

The redesigned digital assets feature a modern, cohesive, and user-friendly experience that aligns with CityFibre's new branding. The successful implementation of the visual identity sets CityFibre apart in the industry.

Impact & Results:

Improved Conversions on Key User Journeys:

The redesign and improved user experience significantly boosted conversions on key user journeys. Users found it easier to navigate the website, locate information, and complete essential tasks, resulting in higher engagement and satisfaction.

Unique Identity in the Market:

The successful implementation of the new branding across CityFibre's digital assets has set them apart from competitors, showcasing their innovative approach and commitment to providing top-notch fibre infrastructure services to their customers.

Increase in New Sign-ups:

New sign-ups for CityFibre services grew by 25% within 1 year of the redesign. The appealing and intuitive user experience encouraged more users to explore CityFibre's services and ultimately sign up. The increase in new sign-ups is a testament to the effectiveness of the redesign in attracting and retaining customers.

Positive Feedback from Client and Stakeholders:

The project received positive feedback from both the client and stakeholders. The redesigned digital assets not only met but exceeded client and stakeholder expectations, garnering praise for the quality of work and attention to detail. The project's success reaffirmed CityFibre's confidence in their digital presence and reinforced the value of excellent design in achieving business objectives.

Learnings:

The CityFibre project taught me the importance of effectively implementing a rebrand while improving the user experience. I honed my skills in translating a company's vision into a compelling digital presence that drives tangible results. This experience has enriched my expertise as a product designer, preparing me to take on even more challenging and impactful projects in the future.
