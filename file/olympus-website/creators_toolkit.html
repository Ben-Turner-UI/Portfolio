Mockup Kit

The Creators toolkit, built to help you design and present faces your way

Using Photoshop, you can edit smart objects and add your own watch faces onto over 10 different 3D mockups, additionally, you can use the pre-designed selection of complications to help you design your own faces.

Bringing 3D to a new medium

Olympus wants creators to have the ability to showcase their watch faces in their best light, that is why Olympus has designed the creators toolkit.

Making mockups easy

Using the built in Sketch file, designers can use the pre-made complications to lay-out their own faces, figuring out new ways to design and wireframe.

Royal National Institute of Blind People

Olympus has decided to support the RNIB with 50% of revenue gained going towards the charity from the Creators Toolkit. The Royal National Institute of Blind People (RNIB), one of the UK’s leading sight loss charities and the largest community of blind and partially sighted people. They’re a catalyst for change, inspiring people with sight loss to transform their own personal experience, their community and, ultimately, society as a whole.

3D Mockups with Photoshop

Using Photoshop designers can superimpose their face onto a 3D mock-up, giving them and their users a new perspective. Within this kit, you will find both the Galaxy Gear and Galaxy Active smartwatch mockups.

UI Elements within Sketch

Using Sketch, designers can leverage the programs tools for creating circular interfaces. It’s regarded as the best tool for creating vector watch faces to develop mock-ups and lay out their faces with pre-made elements.
