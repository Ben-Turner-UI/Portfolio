Using Facer you can download and use Olympus watch faces.

Classic

A remarkable vintage face, with complications showing the heartrate, battery and date. Designed for the traditionalist, inspired by nautical dials and built upon British wartime history, this dial makes an excellent addition to your wrist.

Olympus

Back to the fundamentals, but on white! A traditional and minimalistic face from the foundations of the Olympus brand.

Trinity

Uniquely crafted, the Trinity face offers a space-age dial with strong accents and a tripolar array of complications. Battery, heartrate and the date are all visible, surrounded by rotating particles, which connote the seconds hand on the dial.

Trio

Modernism, combined with purpose, this face was designed with sporting in mind, Trinity uses a tripartite design with three distinct complications to complete it’s functional profile.
