MEASURING CITYFIBRE PORTAL UX AND RESULTS: INNOVATING UX TESTING APPROACHES WITH AI AND AGILE METHODOLOGIES

Client:

CityFibre

Industry:

Broadband Infrastructure

Website:

The CityFibre Unify Portal

Tools:

Typeform, Figma, and ChatGPT.

Project Overview:

CityFibre, a leading UK broadband infrastructure provider, entrusted the challenging task of their entire digital rebranding to DawsonAndrews. As the lead designer, I was responsible for the complete redesign of their marketing website, backend portal, campaign landing pages, company emails, and other digital facets using tools like CraftCMS, Tailwind CSS, and Figma.

Problem Statement:

CityFibre aimed to overhaul its digital image into something more contemporary, inclusive, and challenger. The goals were to enhance user experience, improve accessibility, and differentiate themselves from key industry players like Virgin Media and British Telecom.

Design Process:

Rebranding & Redesign:

Our initial step was updating our design systems within Figma to align with CityFibre's latest brand guidelines, provided by MadeThought. Following this, I redesigned each block of the website to ensure a consistent and eye-catching digital presence.

Research & Ideation:

I thoroughly examined CityFibre's branding objectives to fully understand their vision. This insight empowered me to deliver a rebranding strategy that not only aligned with their vision but also made CityFibre distinguishable in a saturated market.

Implementation & Handover:

Efficiency and compatibility with our key tools, including CraftCMS and Tailwind CSS, were central to the implementation process. I enhanced our design systems and developed a type scale for superior developer handovers. This new system revolutionised our design handover process, leading to a substantial increase in our Jira right-first-time rating from 75% to 95%.

User Experience Improvements:

Aiming to improve accessibility and usability, I managed to implement the brand's challenging neon-dominated colour scheme while adhering to double-A standard accessibility. This balance ensured an engaging, user-friendly experience without compromising on design aesthetics.

Final Solution:

The redesigned digital assets feature a modern, cohesive, and user-friendly experience that aligns with CityFibre's new branding. The successful implementation of the visual identity sets CityFibre apart in the industry.

Impact & Results:

Improved Developer Handovers:

My new design system significantly enhanced the quality of developer handovers, improving our Jira right-first-time rating to 95%. This efficient process change led to smoother project implementation, reducing potential back-and-forth revisions.

Distinctive Brand Identity:

The rebrand's vibrant colour scheme and contemporary aesthetics distinguished CityFibre, making it an eye-catching option for potential customers.

Boost in Business Opportunities:

As an important testament to the success of the rebrand, our contract with CityFibre was extended substantially. This brought increased stability and new opportunities for the company, affirming the effectiveness of our design work and solidifying our relationship with the client.

Learnings:

Leading the CityFibre project highlighted the importance of an efficient and well-executed digital rebrand. By building a design system compatible with our toolset (CraftCMS and Tailwind CSS), I was able to enhance both the development process and the final product. This project has significantly enriched my skillset as a product designer, equipping me to handle larger, more impactful projects in the future.
